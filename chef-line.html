Chef Line's Journey

Work In Progress

Food Themed Action Adventure Game

Description

This project stems from my 1st internship where I had the rare opportunity to work on my own project with my own team of 6 whilst being guided by the Company we we're interning at

The premise of the game is that u are playing as a chef that got transported to a food themed world and where u have to fight or cook ur way through! To solve the mysteries of this world and why U are there!

public class WaveManager : MonoBehaviour

The WaveManager script is responsible for managing the wave-based gameplay, including enemy spawning with their accurate data, videos for the different waves. This makes it really easy for me to adjust and or add waves.
