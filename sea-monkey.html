Sea Monkey

This game was made during a 72-hour game jam in a group of 2 developers and 4 artists. The theme for this jam was reflection.

The premise of the game is to, while in the air, flip the monkey's surfboard and reflect the beach balls the fish is shooting back towards itself. Do this enough times, and you might get a nice surprise at the end!
